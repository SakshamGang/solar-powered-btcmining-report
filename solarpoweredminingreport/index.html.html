Bitcoin Mining Business Report

Setup Description

This report outlines the estimated revenue for a Bitcoin mining operation powered by a solar energy setup. The mining setup consists of 6 Antminer S21 Pro miners, each consuming 3500 watts per hour, equating to 84 kWh per day per miner. For 6 miners, the total daily energy consumption is 504 kWh.

A 5 to 6 ton air conditioning unit is required, consuming approximately 180 kWh per day when running 24 hours.

The solar farm is a 255 kWp setup, generating approximately 3.29 units (kWh) per kW per day, resulting in a total daily energy production of about 838.95 kWh. This provides sufficient energy for the setup, with some excess capacity. Although up to 7 miners could be supported, 6 miners are used for safety.

Mining is conducted in Antpool with a pool fee of 2.5%. Each Antminer S21 Pro has a hash rate of 234 TH/s.
